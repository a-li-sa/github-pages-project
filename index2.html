It's Ocean Sunfish, AKA the Mola Mola

Mola molas are related to puffer fish and triggerfish, but they are the largest bony fish in the ocean. They can be found near the surface of the ocean water, basking in the sun to aid digestion and keep their bodies warm. They are often seen lying on their sides, as if they are playing dead.

Fun Facts

  • Although an unlikely subject for an indie game, the mola mola is the protagonist of the simulation game Survive! Mola Mola! available on iOS and Android.
  • The average mola mola weighs over 2000 pounds. The largest one weighed at 2.5 tons.
  • They were an acceptable form of tax payment to shoguns in Japan during the 1600s and 1700s.
  • In Animal Crossing New Horizons, the following text is displayed when the player catches an ocean sunfish, "I caught an ocean sunfish! Good thing I brought ocean sunscreen!"
